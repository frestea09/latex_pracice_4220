	Belajar simbol matematika pada latex
	
	Tambah : 
	$$ x = a + b$$
	
	Kurang : 
	$$ x = a - b $$
	
	Kali : 
	$$ x = a \times b $$
	
	Bagi : 
	$$ x = a \frac{a}{b} $$
	$$ x = a\div b $$
	
	Akar :
	$$ x = \sqrt{a}$$
	$$ x = \sqrt[2]{x}$$
	
	Pangkat : 
	$$ 2x^{2} $$
	$$ x^{2^2} $$
	
	subscripts : 
	$$ 2x_{2}$$
	
	Greek leters : 
	$$\pi$$
	$$\alpha$$
	$$ x = \pi \times r^2 $$
	
	Trig Function : 
	$$ x = \sin{x}$$
	$$ y = \cos x $$
	$$ z = \tan x $$
